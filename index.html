CURRENT PROJECTS

// Causal ML  ·  Fairness  ·  OOD Generalisation

Causal Deconfounding — Proof of Concept

Demonstrates that a conventional, purely associative deep model silently latches onto a confounder and therefore (a) fails to generalise out-of-distribution and (b) is unfair across patient subgroups. Adding the invariance objective of IRM removes the shortcut, which restores fairness and OOD robustness — all in a self-contained notebook.

github.com/alidotkhalid/causal_deconfounding_poc

// Continual Learning  ·  Neuroscience-Inspired  ·  E/I Balance

E/I-Balanced Dynamic Routing — Proof of Concept

Proof of concept for mitigating catastrophic forgetting using excitatory/inhibitory (E/I) balanced routing. Draws on neuroscience principles to dynamically gate network pathways, preserving previously learned representations while acquiring new tasks.

github.com/alidotkhalid/ei-dynamic-routing-poc
